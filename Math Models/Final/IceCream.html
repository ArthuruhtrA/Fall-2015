Ice Cream Ordered Puzzle

Four girls, Bridget, Glenda, Patricia and Viola, go to the ice cream store to order a cone with one scoop of ice cream. Each girl has a unique favorite flavor: Cherry, Coconut, Peppermint or Vanilla. On entering the store, the girls line up to place their orders.

Below are some facts applicable to the problem, use these and the constraint that no two distinct girls have the same favorite flavor, to determine which flavor each girl ordered and where each girl was in line. NOTE: In the Alloy model the girls are ordered from first to last, where the first girl is the first one to place an order.

1.    The second person in line is either Glenda or the person who ordered a scoop of Peppermint.

2.    The four customers are exactly

  1. The second person in line,
  2. Glenda,
  3. Bridget
  4. The person who ordered a Vanilla scoop.

3.    Viola was 2 spots in line behind the person who ordered a scoop of Coconut.

4.    Glenda ordered a scoop of Cherry ice cream.
